What Prompt Bank Does

Prompt Bank is built for people who iterate on prompts regularly and want a reliable way to keep their best versions organized. Instead of copying prompts into scattered notes or searching old chats, you can store them once and retrieve them in seconds.

  • Save prompts with optional title, tags, and source.
  • Search by meaning (semantic search) to find relevant prompts even if you don’t remember the exact words.
  • Keep it personal with per-user storage so your library stays separated and organized.
  • Reuse confidently by maintaining a single, consistent prompt library over time.
