Privacy Policy

Prompt Bank

Last updated:

Summary

Prompt Bank stores prompts you choose to save and uses vector embeddings to help you search and retrieve them.

Information We Collect

  • Saved prompts and metadata (e.g., title, tags, source, timestamps) that you submit.
  • Authentication identifiers (e.g., an Auth0 user ID) to associate prompts with your account.
  • Operational data (limited request/response metadata and error logs) for security and reliability.

How We Use Information

  • To provide prompt storage, search, and retrieval features.
  • To secure the service and prevent abuse.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve reliability.

Data Processing & Storage

  • To enable semantic search, your prompt text may be processed to create an embedding using AWS Bedrock.
  • Embeddings and prompt metadata may be stored in AWS S3 Vectors.

Retention and Deletion

Prompts remain stored until you delete them or the service is discontinued. If you request account deletion, we will delete your stored prompts and associated vectors within a reasonable period, subject to legal and operational requirements.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers and infrastructure providers used to operate the service (for example, AWS and authentication providers).

Your Choices

  • You can delete saved prompts from within the product experience.
  • You can contact us to request deletion of your account data.

Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect data, but no system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

Children’s Privacy

The service is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
