Privacy Policy

Prompt Bank

Last updated:

Summary

Prompt Bank stores prompts you choose to save and uses vector embeddings to help you search and retrieve them.

Information We Collect

  • Saved prompts and metadata (title, tags, source, timestamps) that you submit.
  • Authentication identifiers (for example, an Auth0 user subject ID) to separate user data.
  • Operational logs (limited) for debugging and reliability.

How We Use Information

  • To provide prompt storage, search, and retrieval features.
  • To secure the service and prevent abuse.
  • To operate and improve reliability.

Data Processing & Storage

  • Prompts may be embedded using an AWS Bedrock embeddings model.
  • Embeddings and metadata may be stored in AWS S3 Vectors.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers needed to operate the app.

Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect data, but no system can be guaranteed 100% secure.
